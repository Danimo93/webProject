Cycle Sport

Cycle Sport was a cycling magazine published 13 times a year by IPC Media. The magazine was first published in June 1993.[2] It was edited by Robert Garbutt.[3] In July 2016, it was announced that the magazine was to closed down,[4] with the final issue being September 2016.[5] The magazine and Cycling Active were incorporated into Cycling Weekly, which is also published by the same company
